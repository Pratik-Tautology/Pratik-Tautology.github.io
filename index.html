ABOUT THE PROJECT


This project is part of [CSP203] at IIT ROPAR. Objective of this course is to train students to use unix/programming environment more efficiently to write better software and faster. Project carries 50% of the total marks of this course. This course is being taught by Dr. Mukesh Saini and Dr. Neerja Goel (assistant professor @ IIT Ropar in CS Department) and TA for this course is Kaneez Fizza. 10 groups each consisting of 4 members are being made which will make 10 different projects. Students are supposed to propose a project that could be implemented in C/C++/Java and descibe its end goal, target audience, features, shortcomings, limitations, etc.
